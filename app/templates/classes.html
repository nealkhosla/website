Classes

Here, I'll try to objectively chronicle what different classes I've taken at Stanford are like - I'll add more information to this as I get more time so that other students can know what to expect from classes. If you're a student reading this and want more information on one of these classes (or if you teach one of these classes), feel free to contact me. I'll start by noting that generally when I take classes I'm motivated by a few main things: how much I learn, how much it develops my critical thinking/problem solving ability, whether or not I could've, and more importantly would've, learned the material on my own, and how interesting the material was to me.
