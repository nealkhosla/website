STATS202 - I don't have that much to say about this class as it is pretty straightforward and didn't require too much work or effort. I thought it was fairly easy as it didn't really require me to understand more of the theory and concepts behind data mining techniques but just how to implement them in R mostly. For me, it was my first time using R so I learned that, and it was a nice introduction to some techniques in data mining that further courses would cover in more depth (particularly CS229), but on the whole it wasn't that useful. I took the class with a visiting professor from Google so I'm not sure if it changes a lot during the year (I hope it does) but I've heard it's still fairly easy.
